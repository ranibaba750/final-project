Manchester City Football Club

About

I began supporting Manchester City Football Club during the 2012-13 season. At the time, Yaya Toure was playing and I really enjoyed watching him. His playstyle really enticed me to keep watching Man City every weekend. Ever since then, I have been loyal to the club as it has been the only soccer club I have supported in my life.

Favorite Season

My favorite Man City season is the 2020-21 season. In this season, we won the Premier League and EFL Cup, came second place in the Champions League, and reached the semi-finals of the FA Cup. We also had a 28 game unbeaten streak in all competitions with including 21 straight wins. My favorite wins came against Paris Saint-Germain in the semi-finals of the UEFA Champions League where we won 4-1 on aggregate.

Favorite Team

My favorite Man City team is the 2013-14 team. This team featured some of my favorite players including Yaya Toure, Sergio Aguero, David Silva, Vincent Kompany, Joe Hart, and Fernandinho. This team also won the Premier League title after a close race against Liverpool FC.

Favorite Jerseys

Throughout the years, I have really liked the jerseys Man City have worn in-game. It was hard to choose my favorites, but I ended up choosing these 6: 2013-14 home, 2012-13 away, 2017-18 away, 2020-21 away, 2021-22 away, and 2022-23 away.

Favorite Player

My favorite Man City player is Yaya Toure. Toure played at Man City from 2010-2018. As a midfielder, he was crucial in helping Man City win 7 trophies during his time there. Because of him, I became a fan of the club.
